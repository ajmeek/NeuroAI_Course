Intro#

Prerequisites#

We assume you have completed the equivalent of the computational neuroscience (CN) and DL courses:

  • Computational Neuroscience

  • Deep Learning

Well extensively use transformers and attention in the first lesson and cover neural data analysis (in particular EMG) in the second tutorial.

Please review the precourse materials if necessary!
